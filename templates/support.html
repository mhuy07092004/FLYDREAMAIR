Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my flight?

expand_more

You can change your flight through our website or mobile app. Log in to your account, go to "My Bookings", select the flight you want to change, and follow the instructions.

What is the baggage allowance?

expand_more

Baggage allowance varies by ticket type and route. Economy class typically includes one checked bag up to 23kg and one carry-on bag up to 7kg. Please check your booking confirmation for specific details.

How do I request a refund?

expand_more

Refund requests can be made through our website or by contacting customer service. Please have your booking reference number ready. Refund eligibility depends on your ticket type and the time of cancellation.
